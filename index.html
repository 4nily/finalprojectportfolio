Project 1

Injected Ideals

Artist Statement

My piece addresses the beauty standards imposed by social media through the information we consume. I am trying to communicate with others who utilize social media platforms in their everyday lives to warn them and individuals who are intoxicated by comparison. My piece underscores the juxtaposition of oneself and others through the act of mindlessly scrolling on their phone. People consume and process information with expectations and beauty standards from social media to invent the perfect perception they want for themselves.

Project 2

Time Moves Along

Artist Statement

My video project is composed of captured moments from my digital camera during the 2025-2026 college year. Though the videos presented are primarily during the college year, the intended audience can be anyone. The narration over the video clips reflects themes of the time and creating fond memories, to convey the importance of being present while living and the minuscule moments missed when one is not paying attention to what is right in front of them.
